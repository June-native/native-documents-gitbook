### Integration Partners

For integrators routing through Native Relay, we collaborate with partners such as aggregators, wallets, and other taker/flow sources to establish customized fee rates based on individual requirements.

Please note that standard maker and taker fee rates are applied. However, there is typically zero or minimal additional Relay fee to maintain a competitive offering in the market.&#x20;
