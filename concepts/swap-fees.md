### Integration Partners

For integrators routing through Native Relay, we collaborate with partners such as aggregators, wallets, and other taker/flow sources to establish customized fee rates based on individual requirements. Typically additional Relay fee is set at zero or minimal to maintain a competitive offering in the market.&#x20;

Please note that standard maker and taker fee rates are still applied in Relay.
