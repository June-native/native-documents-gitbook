# Single-Sided Liquidity Pools

The **Native Credit Pool** allows single-token liquidity provision, offering:

* **No impermanent loss**: Even with multiple liquidity positions, the token balance ratio remain unchanged while each of them accrues yields.
* **Minimal maintenance**: No rebalancing or adjustments are necessary; your liquidity will be automatically paired and utilised in the most efficient manner.
* **Multiple reward streams**: Earn yields from PMM's interests, and swap fees from trades that utilise the liquidity you deposited.
