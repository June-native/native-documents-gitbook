# Base and Listed Assets

Assets are categorized as:

* **Base Assets**: Large-cap tokens (e.g., USDT, USDC, WBTC, WETH).
* **Listed Assets**: Tokens from established protocols (e.g., STONE, mETH, stBTC).

Liquidity providers can add single-sided liquidity to base or listed assets. Furthermore, additional dedicated pairing options are exclusively accessible when adding liquidity to base assets.
