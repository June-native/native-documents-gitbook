# Pair Liquidity

**To Add and Pair Liquidity on Native:**

1. After connecting your wallet, choose Network you want to use

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (69).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. Click “Add & Pair Liquidity” button

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/5 (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. Select the base token and listed token, type the amount that you want to deposit

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/6.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4. Check the APY including pairing incentive APR and click “Add and Pair” button

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/7 (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You get to enjoy “pairing incentive APR” by token issuers given as a reward for your base token dedicated pairing

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/8 (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### **To Check your Liquidity Status on Native:**

You can check the current liquidity status through each token’s view page

1. Click “View” icon of WETH

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/9.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. You can view the current pairing status of your WETH and its APY

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/10.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. If you want to remove your liquidity, click “-” icon from the status page to withdraw

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/11.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
